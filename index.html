"Girl1 RPG" is a traditional turn-based RPG that features mechanics seen in various Atlus and Final Fantasy titles. Master the art of "Press Turn" combat, level up to acquire new powers and skills, and do whatever it takes to save the planet.

"Girl1 RPG" will have the vibe and structure of classic 90's JRPGs mixed with a combat system and story that more closely resembles modern JRPGs.
